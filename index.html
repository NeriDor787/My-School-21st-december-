Welcome to Redeemers International School Enugu, where excellence in education meets a nurturing and inclusive community. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower the leaders of tomorrow. At Redeemers International School, we prioritize the holistic development of our students. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to providing a rich and engaging learning experience. We offer a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and support services to ensure that each student reaches their full potential. Located in the vibrant city of Enugu, our school provides a safe and inspiring environment for students to learn and grow. We believe in fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a sense of responsibility in our students, preparing them to excel in a rapidly changing world.
